Pandemic Deviations from Expected US Hobby Interests

How the pandemic changed our interests through the lens of Google searches.

How to use this chart:

  • The heatmap reports difference between actual and expected Google search volumes based on season-trend regression models fitted to 5 years of historical Google Trends data prior to March 2020.
  • The differences range from -100 to +100, with negative and positive values respectively indicating decreased and increased interest relative to pre-pandemic expectations.
  • Exact months and values will display based on mouse movement over the tiles.
