Pandemic Deviations from Expected U.S. Hobby Interests

How the pandemic changed our interests through the lens of Google searches.

How to use this chart:

  • The heatmap reports difference between actual Google search volumes and expected search volumes, based on statistical models estimated using 5 years of data prior to March 2020.
  • The differences range from -100 to +100. Negative (positive) values indicate decreased (increased) interest relative to pre-pandemic trends.
  • Exact months and values are available by hovering over the tiles with your mouse.
